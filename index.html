I like cloud saves for games. However many games I play don't have the luxury of Steam or Origin Cloud Saving. Without a service, playing a game on my Desktop, and then continuing where I left off on my laptop is a tedious task since I have to send files from my Desktop to my Laptop. Save Sync automates the process by smartly managing data.
